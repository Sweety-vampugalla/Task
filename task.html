Welcome to Desi Travels

Welcome to Desi Travels – your gateway to exploring the incredible beauty and culture of India. From the iconic Taj Mahal in Agra to the peaceful backwaters of Kerala, and from the snowy mountains of Leh-Ladakh to the vibrant streets of Jaipur, India is full of unforgettable destinations. Whether you’re seeking adventure, spirituality, relaxation, or rich heritage, Desi Travels will guide you through the top tourist places, local experiences, and travel tips to make your journey truly special.

Top 10 Tourist Places in India

Taj Mahal, Agra

A symbol of eternal love, and one of the 7 Wonders of the World.

Leh-Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir

Snow-capped mountains and scenic beauty perfect for adventure lovers.

Jaipur, Rajasthan

The Pink City with palaces, forts, and colorful markets.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

The spiritual heart of India with sacred rivers and ancient rituals.

Kerala Backwaters

Peaceful houseboat rides through lush green landscapes and lagoons.

Goa

Famous for beaches, sunsets, and vibrant nightlife.

Ranthambore National Park

Spot wild tigers and enjoy nature safaris.

Mysore, Karnataka

Royal history and the stunning Mysore Palace.

Darjeeling, West Bengal

Tea gardens and panoramic Himalayan views.

Charminar

A historic monument and mosque known for its grand arches and bustling market streets.
